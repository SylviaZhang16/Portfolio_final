Security Deposit

Security Deposit of $400 is required at the time of application. A $75 non-refundable application fee is applicable.

Pet Policy

We allow indoor cats and dogs for an additional charge of $35 per month for one or $60 per month for two (max), along with a one-time $350 non-refundable pet fee. Breed restrictions and weight limits apply. No aggressive breeds. No exotic animals. Pet policy is not negotiable.

Refer a Neighbor

Island Drive Apartments offers a $200 fee for each referral. If you are a leaseholder at Island Drive Apartments and refer a new neighbor, who signs a 12-month lease; upon occupancy by the new neighbor we will credit you the referral fee. In addition, the referred neighbor will receive $100 credit upon occupancy.
